01 / About

Eyez like a lens.

"Your favourite photographer's favourite photographer. I shoot the night, the vows, the brand, the face. Same eye, every time."

EyezLikeLenz is a photography practice based in London and working across the UK. The work spans weddings, parties, fashion editorial, and brand campaigns, with a consistent attention to atmosphere, light, and feeling.

Clients come for the look. They come back for the turnaround and the way it feels on the night. Every shoot is approached as its own story, never a template.
